The Author

Ryan North

I've been writing professionally for almost a decade. Dinosaur Comics (a fixed-art comic where the pictures don't change but the words do) brought me early recognition, and more recently the Adventure Time comics I write have been super popular, going to second and even third printings. This is my first novel, and I think it's my best work yet.

I've included a bunch of links below where you can see other writing, from my Twitter account (jokes! And cranky complaints sometimes too!) to my B^F blog, where I reviewed the insane novelization of the Back To The Future movie, page-by-page, I think you'll find something that makes you say "Man, I want to read more from this guy, but in a chooseable-path gamebook format. Also, there should be explosions in the book."

I also included a link to my Wikipedia page so that you can see I am a Legit Dude with a history, and not one of those terrible fly-by-night interactive fiction authors your mother warned you about.
